<topics>
1. Thyroid gland: The thyroid gland plays a crucial role in regulating metabolism and overall body functions through the secretion of hormones T3 and T4.

2. Parathyroid glands: The parathyroid glands work in concert with the thyroid gland to maintain calcium homeostasis in the body by secreting parathyroid hormone (PTH).

3. Pancreas: The pancreas has both digestive and endocrine functions, secreting insulin and glucagon to regulate blood sugar levels.

4. Adrenal glands: The adrenal glands, consisting of the medulla and cortex, are involved in the stress response and secrete various hormones like cortisol, DHEA, and aldosterone.

5. Ovaries and testes: The ovaries and testes are stimulated by gonadotropic hormones (FSH and LH) from the pituitary gland to secrete sex hormones like estrogen and testosterone.
</topics>
