<summary>
In this lecture, Mike Bridger discusses remedy mapping, focusing on the homeopathic remedy Pulsatilla. He begins by recapping the remedies covered in previous lectures and their relationships to each other. He then delves into the details of Pulsatilla, discussing its changeability, its lack of depth in terms of pathology, and its hormonal and circulatory effects.
