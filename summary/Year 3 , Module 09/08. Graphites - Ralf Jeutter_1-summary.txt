<summary>
In this lecture, Ralf Jeutter discusses the homeopathic remedy Graphites, which he believes is an under-prescribed remedy. Graphites is an antipsoric remedy that is useful in cases of suppressed skin eruptions. The remedy is suitable for people with a tendency towards obesity, delayed menses, and a cold, timid, and sluggish nature. The key physical symptoms of Graphites include cracks, fissures, dryness, and bleeding of the skin, as well as a tendency towards obesity, constipation, and a lack of stamina. The key mental symptoms include extreme caution, indecision, and a tendency towards sadness and despondency. Jeutter presents several cases where Graphites was successfully prescribed based on the patient's symptomatology and discusses the remedy's relationships with other remedies such as Lycopodium, Pulsatilla, and Calcarea.
</summary>
