<summary>
In this lecture, Marcus Fernandez discusses the importance of embodying homeopathy as a practitioner. He emphasizes the need to be present with patients, to listen deeply, and to perceive what needs to be cured in each case. Fernandez also touches on the challenges of the modern world, including the distractions of technology and the lack of deep thinking. He encourages students to keep their homeopathy simple, to go back to the basics, and to focus on their passion for the subject. Fernandez also addresses the challenges of skepticism towards homeopathy and encourages students to make informed choices and to support their patients in their healthcare decisions. Throughout the lecture, Fernandez stresses the importance of embodying homeopathy, having impact, and building a livelihood as a practitioner.
</summary>
