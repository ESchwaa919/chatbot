<recommendations>
1. Patients with contact dermatitis should be advised to avoid the identified irritants or allergens and to use protective measures, such as gloves, when necessary.
2. Topical steroids are the first-choice treatment for contact dermatitis, but patients should be monitored for potential side effects, such as skin thinning and increased susceptibility to infections.
3. Patients with urticaria should be advised to identify and avoid triggers, and to seek prompt medical attention if pharyngeal involvement occurs.
</recommendations>
